v1.8.0
- Dynamic FPS: Default FPS is now 60 to reduce flicker, and it is automatically adjusted down on complex frames to keep scanning accuracy. You might have to re-adjust your optimization settings.
- Support for ILDA Digital Network (IDN) DACs.
- Modification sliders in Live Mode to adjust the color and effects of all playing files on the fly.
- Misc. other fixes and features like alternative Tab key behavior and onion skinning in Live Mode.

v1.7.0
- New Live Mode: Play files on the fly, for DJs and improvised shows.
